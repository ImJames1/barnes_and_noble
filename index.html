About Us

Barnes & Noble is a bookstore that sells books, magazines, newspapers, and more. The company's history traces back to 1873, when Charles M. Barnes started a book company in Wheaton, Illinois. His son, William Barnes, traveled to New York and joined G. Clifford Noble to create Barnes & Noble. Today, Barnes & Noble is the largest retail bookseller in the United States, and has 600 bookstores across all 50 states.

Bestsellers

The Let Them Theory: A Life-Changing Tool That Millions of People Can't Stop Talking About

'The Let Them Theory' is a self-help book written by Mel Robbins. Robbins returns to the art of literature with a step-by-step guide on how to focus on what you can control and letting go of what you cannot control. Robbins teaches her readers that releasing the need to manage everyone and everything can improve their lives.

Sunrise on the Reaping (B&N Exclusive Edition) (A Hunger Games Novel)

'Sunrise on the Reaping' is a science fiction, dystopian novel written by Susan Collins. Collins takes readers back to a young Haymitch Abernathy in this prequel novel in the "Hunger Games" series. The story focuses on Haymitch as he is forced to compete in the 50th Hunger Games, where double the amount of tributes are selected from each district to compete in the Games.

The Court of the Dead: A Nico di Angelo Adventure (Signed B&N Exclusive Edition)

'The Court of the Dead: A Nico di Angelo Adventure' is a fantasy-adventure novel written by Rick Riordan and Mark Oshiro. The story follows Nico di Angelo and Will Solace as they investigate a dark force in the Underworld that is targeting and punishing monsters for their past crimes.

Tell Me What You Did: A Novel

'Tell Me What You Did: A Novel' is a psychological thriller written by Carter Wilson. Wilson introduces readers to Poe Webb, the host of popular true crime podcast who invites people to anonymously confess their crimes to her audience.

Fourth Wing

'Fourth Wing' is a high fantasy romance novel written by Rebecca Yarros. Yarros welcomes readers to the life of Violet Sorrengail, a young woman who is ordered by her mother to join the dragon-riding Quadrant at Basgiath War College.
